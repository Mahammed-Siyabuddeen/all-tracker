    About Company

    ALLTRACKER, LLC has been a leader in providing professional uniformed security service since 1990. Since our inception we have developed an excellent reputation in providing State of the Art Security Services to meet commercial, residential and industrial needs. The President and Founder of the company has been a licensed holder since 1985 and has over 24 years experience in Law Enforcement. AllTracker, LLC has developed into a reputable and diverse full service security provider with satellite offices in Detroit, Lansing, Traverse City and Westland.
